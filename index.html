Instructions

      

        Player functions:


        moveRight() - Moves 1 space right

        moveLeft() - Moves 1 space left

        moveUp() - Moves 1 space up

        moveDown() - Moves 1 space down

        shootBall() - Shoot ball 4 spaces right



        1st Level: The user has to move right a certain number
        of times to get closer to the goal and shoot the ball to score with no
        defenders.



        2nd Level: 2 defenders in front of goal. User starts in
        the middle left of screen. They move around both defenders and then back
        in front of goal to shoot and score. No loops required.



        3rd Level: More difficult level. User starts on the
        left side of screen and needs to move to the right until in front of a
        defender where they move around and score. Introduces while loops and
        the new function inFront().



        4th Level: Introduce moving defenders. Defenders move
        only up and down. After moving through all defenders the user shoots the
        ball to score.

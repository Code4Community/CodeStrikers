Levels

        

          
Level 1

          

            Move right until close to the goal and shoot to score. No defenders.
          

        


        

          
Level 2

          

            Two defenders in front of the goal. Move around them, then shoot to
            score (no loops required).
          

        


        

          
Level 3

          

            More difficult level introducing while loops and
            inFront(). Move around defenders to score.
          

        


        

          
Level 4

          

            Defenders move up and down. Time your movement and shoot to score!
          

        


        

          
Level 5

          

            Advanced level with complex defender patterns. Master your coding
            skills!
